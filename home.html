My Projects

Electricity Generating Wrist Wearable

This innovative wrist wearable generates electricity from everyday hand movements, providing a sustainable energy source for small devices.

  • Dual Energy Harvesting Mechanisms:
    • Piezoelectric Transducers: Convert mechanical stress from hand gestures into electrical energy.
    • Magnetic Induction: Utilizes neodymium magnets and a copper solenoid for energy generation.
  • Energy Storage: Compact, efficient battery ensures continuous power availability.
  • Seamless Integration: Lightweight, ergonomic design combines all technologies.

Impact: Feasible and sustainable energy generation reducing dependence on conventional sources.

Applications: Ideal for small devices like fitness trackers or smartwatches.
